About

WHO: 7th and 8th graders who love math and are looking for math enrichment at their school!

WHAT: Math Circle is a weekly meeting of students who enjoy mathematics and want the added challenge of learning exciting topics outside of the standard curriculum! Math challenges will be given on a weekly basis that students can complete on their own time. The group will be organized and run by a Willard graduate who is now attending Berkeley High School.

WHEN / WHERE: YSP office, every Monday during lunch beginning October 7th!
